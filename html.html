Here's a timeline of Warren Buffets life

  • 1930 - Born in Omaha, Nebraska
  • 1937 - Age 7, read book "One Thousand Ways to Make $1000".
  • 1941 - Age 11, bought first stock, Citgo Petroleum Service .
  • 1943 - Age 14, bought land with $1,200 of his savings.
  • 1945 - Sophmore in highschool, started a pinball machine business.
  • 1947 - Entered Wharton School of University of Pennsylvania.
  • 1951 - Graduated with Masters of Science in Economics from Columbia University.
  • 1951 - Worked as Investment Salesman at Buffett-Falk & Co.
  • 1952 - Married Susan Thompson.
  • 1954 - Worked as Securities Analyst at Graham-Newman Corp.
  • 1956 - Worked as General Partner at Buffett Partnership, Ltd
  • 1970 - Chairman and CEO at Berkshire Hathaway Inc.
  • 1990 - Became a billionaire when Berkshire Hathaway started selling Class A shares.
  • 2008 - Financial Crisis that shocked the world.
  • 2008 - Richest person in the world at $62 billion net worth.
  • 2014 - Berkshire Hathaway Inc. made $6.4 billion in net profit, most it made in 3-month period.
